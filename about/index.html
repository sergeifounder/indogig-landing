Our Story

From a staffing gap to a staffing platform

Sergei Krushinskikh moved to Bali and quickly noticed a recurring problem: hotels, beach clubs, and event venues were scrambling to fill shifts at the last minute. They relied on word-of-mouth, scattered WhatsApp groups, and middlemen who added cost without accountability.

IndoGig started as a simple idea: what if businesses could request verified, insured workers through a single WhatsApp message and have shifts filled within hours? No downloads needed. No complicated onboarding. Just fast, compliant staffing built around the tools Bali already uses.

Today, PT INDOGIG HRTECH INDONESIA is a fully registered Indonesian company. Every worker is covered under labor compliance. Every placement is documented. We operate across Seminyak, Canggu, Kuta, Ubud, Nusa Dua, and beyond — serving hotels, restaurants, beach clubs, weddings, and large-scale events.
