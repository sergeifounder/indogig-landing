FAQ

Pricing questions

How much does it cost?expand_more
Pricing is per-shift, based on role and duration. Contact us via WhatsApp for a quote tailored to your needs.
Are there hidden fees?expand_more
No. The quoted per-shift fee includes worker pay, insurance, vetting, and our service. Nothing extra.
How do I pay?expand_more
Weekly bank transfer. You receive an itemized invoice every Monday for the previous week's shifts.
Is there a minimum order?expand_more
No. Book one shift or one hundred. No contracts, no minimums.
Do workers pay anything?expand_more
No. IndoGig is completely free for workers.
