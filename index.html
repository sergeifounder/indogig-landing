How fast can I get staff? expand_more
Most requests are filled within 4 hours. For large events (20+ staff), we recommend 48-72 hours notice.
How does pricing work? expand_more
Transparent per-shift fee based on role and duration. No monthly minimum. No hidden charges. Weekly invoicing.
Are workers insured? expand_more
Yes. Every worker placed through IndoGig is covered by labor compliance.
What areas in Bali do you cover? expand_more
Seminyak, Canggu, Kuta, Ubud, Denpasar, Nusa Dua, Jimbaran, and Sanur.
Can I re-book the same workers? expand_more
Yes. Favorite workers get first priority for your bookings. Ideal for recurring shifts.
Do I need to download an app? expand_more
No. Everything runs through WhatsApp. Dashboards and check-in open as web links.
How do workers get paid? expand_more
Weekly bank transfer to their registered account. Fully tracked and transparent.
